# Culture

This repository stores the main culture documents and playbooks used by the Engineering and Product teams at Superfiliate.

The goal is to keep a shared reference for how we work, what we value, and the principles that guide our decisions, collaboration, and team standards.

## Documents

- [`tech-vision.md`](./tech-vision.md): engineering principles and technical direction
- [`company-values.md`](./company-values.md): the company values that shape how we work together
- [`playbook.md`](./playbook.md): practical guidance and team playbooks
