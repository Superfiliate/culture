# Values

You'll become familiar with five values as they form the foundation of Superfiliate culture. More than just pretty words on a board, these values are the beliefs that we, as a team, stand for. It is how we act and the skills we care about most. They also keep us focused on where we want to go and how we get together - often with many cold brews and karaoke singing along the way.

**So, to keep it simple: this is how we roll.**

**If these values align with your own, and if we sound like the kind of people you want to be surrounded by, join our team and prepare for an exciting and meaningful adventure with us.**

## 1. Say It Like You Mean It

### We think feedback is a gift.

Instead of being an occasional formal - and dull - exercise, honest and constructive feedback is a continuous part of how we communicate and work with one another. It is not just how we identify where problems are and how we can solve them; we believe it is how we can improve and become the best version of ourselves and our team. By constantly delivering our message with empathy and good intent, we make feedback something to be craved rather than something to be feared.

### Rise and shine your opinions!

We believe that every point of view has the power to unlock good ideas and help us bring new solutions to the table. We encourage an environment where the best ideas, not the loudest voices, win; if you’re in a product and have an idea about sales, share it! There is no monopoly or hierarchy on good insights, as we believe that a variety of perspectives and different backgrounds are the only way to get to something powerful and innovative. We believe in having strong opinions, loosely held.

### Trust and accountability

We trust each other and empower everyone with the freedom, power, and support to do what is best for themselves and the company. That being said, this generates a sense of responsibility and self-discipline, to be honest and accountable in every decision.

## 2. Better Together

As we are on the same boat and have the same end goal, we know that helping each other is how we go farther and faster. So we expect everyone in our team always to be a helping hand whenever needed - or not - and to understand and feel that we are in a support system. So no place for lone wolves here; we are a whole pack aiming for the moon! 🚀

## 3. We Are Serious About Having Fun

Yes, we love work, but we also love a good laugh, so we don’t take ourselves too seriously. We believe a playful environment fuels good ideas, better outcomes, and stronger relationships. And we must say, we are mastering the art of good (and healthy) pranks and funny screenshots.

We also celebrate the wins - our work is never done, but we make sure to recognize the success of each other and the team daily, always with a good and loud “Rah rah”! ⚡️

## 4. Go Crazy, Innovate

### No limits to curiosity

We cultivate curiosity as we like to think outside the box and try new things. We share discoveries daily, even outside our field or just random news, because we know that everything has the potential to spark a good idea.

### No risks, no story

We prefer to see things as they are and as they could be. As long as we're constantly making grounded and informed decisions, we're not afraid to make a bet, as we know that everything is an opportunity to learn a valuable lesson that's going to help us get one step closer to a product that is relevant, interesting, easy to use and easy to scale. We are not afraid of failure because it best indicates that we are trying something new and welcoming change.

## 5. 1% Better Every Day

What can 5 minutes a day do? Not much. But applied consistently over a week, month, and year, it could make a radical difference in our work, life, and culture!

So instead of trying to make radical changes quickly, we prefer to make minor but consistent daily improvements that will gradually lead to something significant.

**We don't want to preserve our culture — we want to improve it, slowly but effectively, by 1% every day!**
