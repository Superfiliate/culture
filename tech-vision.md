# Tech Vision

## Engineering Excellence

- **High Standards & Quality Focus:** Our team is committed to high standards, reflected in efforts to maintain low downtime and actively work on performance improvements. The aspiration for continuous improvement and refactoring within development cycles is highly valued.
- **Code Review Processes:** Code reviews are seen as a key practice, supported by a robust CI/CD pipeline that leverages tools like linters, automated tests, and preview environments.
- **Technical Debt Management:** There's an awareness and structured approach starting with a Technical Debt Maturity Model. The dedicated "Chapter Day" is valued as a time for codebase cleanup, as well as a place to focus on internal tech discussions.
- **Design System:** Our team sees this as a key component of our stack. It significantly helps developer experience, acts as a unified language between Engineering and Design, and is seen as a long-term framework for consistent growth of our UI.
- **Modern & productive tech stack:** We embrace the AI empowerment and the usage of AI is highly incentivized, and teams have access to AI tools to improve their workflow. We keep our libraries and frameworks updated, making sure we’re using the latest technologies.

## Learn & Share

- **Internal Team Collaboration:** We are constantly learning and pushing ourselves to be better. We find the space to try something new, find the time to teach someone else something, and find the opportunities to get together to discuss it.
- **Effective documentation:** We use Loom videos and ADRs to define and document patterns across our codebase so other developers can easily refer to them when making a decision.
- **Mentorship:** Our team has a strong mentorship culture where each new joiner is assigned to be mentored by a more experience college. Accessing and talking to specialists in different areas is encouraged and supported.
- **Community**: We continually engage in conferences, produce blog posts, and share our knowledge within our team and with the wider community.

## Product Minded

- **First the why:** We actively seek context and partner with our Product team to understand the 'why' behind what we build, ensuring our technical solutions align with strategic goals and user needs. We proactively participate in conversations with users and stakeholders as an opportunity to learn more about the why and get motivated.
- **Know your game:** We stay informed about our market, competitive landscape, and business objectives to contribute meaningfully to product strategy and technical decision-making.
- **Data-driven:** We leverage data and analytics to drive engineering choices, measure impact, and collaborate with Product on defining and tracking key success metrics for the product's health and evolution.
- **Responsiveness with merchants:** our solutions squad is seen as a valuable and stable entity, always ready to help merchants to have success with our product from a technical point of view. Getting a quick response, and tactical solves to any problems.
